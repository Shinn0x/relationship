Love Story

Chaw

The Girl-Friend

We first met on social media, just two strangers having simple conversations. At first, it was nothing more than casual chats, but somehow, I started looking forward to his messages every day. Then one day, he said, “Let me treat you to some snacks.” It sounded so simple and harmless, but that small invitation became the start of everything. From that moment on, our story began — unexpectedly, naturally, and beautifully.

Shinn

The Boy-Friend

When I first met her, I was completely captivated. She was so beautiful that for a moment, everything else around me faded away. I remember thinking, “Wow… I don’t need anything else, I just need her in my life.” From that very first moment, I knew she was someone special — someone I didn’t want to lose.
